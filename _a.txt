UNIT 1: Meeting People
AONAD 1: Bualadh le Daoine

Key Sounds:

mise (slender s) = I, me
slán (broad s) = goodbye
duit (slender t) = to you
leat (broad t) = with you
muid (slender d) = we, us
siad (broad d) = they


(((1.1) 
Dia duit = Hello (literally, God to_you)
Dia is Muire duit = Hello (literally, God and Mary to_you)
Dia daoibh = Hello (literally, God to_yous {plural})


(((1.2)
Cén chaoi a bhfuil tú? = How are you? (chaoi/caoi = way)(cén chaoi = how)
(fuil= dependant form of tá=to_be, used IN QUESTIONS)

Cad é mar atá tú? = How are you? (Ulster dialect) (mar = way, so, as)

Conas atá tú? = How are you? (Munster dialect)


(((1.3)
Tá mé go maith. = I'm well
Tá mé go breá. = I'm fine

(((1.4)
Is mise Sharon. = I'm Sharon.
Is mise Dónall. = I'm Dónall.
Mise Siobhán = I'm Siobhán


(((1.5)
Máirtín is ainm dom. = Máirtín is name of_mine. (dom = to_me)
Sinéad is ainm dom. = Sinéad is name of_mine.
Peadar is ainm domsa. = Peadar is name of_mine. (domsa = to_ME)
Agus Bríd is ainm domsa. = And Bríd is name of_mine. 


(((1.6)
Cén t-ainm atá ort? = What name is on_you? (Connacht dialect)
/Ken tanm atort?/ (ort = on_you)

Cad is ainm duit? = What is name to_you? (Munster dialect)
/Kad es anm dyth?/ (duit = to_you)

C'ainm atá ort? = What_name is on_you? (Ulster dialect)
/Kanim atort?/

Máirtín is ainm domsa. Cén t-ainm atá ortsa? (ortsa = on_YOU)


(((Conversation 1A)
Michelle: Cén chaoi a bhfuil tú? Is mise Michelle - cén t-ainm atá ortsa?
 = How are you? I'm Michelle - what's YOUR name?
 
Eoin: Mise Eoin. = I'm Eoin.

Michelle: Tá sé go deas bualadh leat, a Eoin. = It's nice to meet with_you Eoin.
(leat = with_you)


(((Conversation 1B)
Áine: Dia duit, a Liam. = Hello Liam.

Liam: Dia is Muire duit, a Áine. Cén chaoi a bhfuil tú na laethanta seo? 
/.... lehynta sio/ (seo = anseo = here <> sin = ansin = there)
= Hello Áine. How are you these days? (lae = day, laethanta = days)

Áine: Tá mé go maith, go raibh maith agat. Agus tú féin?
= I'm well, thank you. And yourself? (maith = good, tú=you, féin=self)
( mé féin = myself )

Liam: Tá mé go breá. Tá sé go deas tú a fheiceáil arís!
=I'm fine. It's nice to see you again (feic = to see, f(h)eiceáil = seen)


(((1.7)
Tá sé go deas bualadh leat, a Eoin. = It's nice to meet you Eoin.

Tá sé go deas bualadh leatsa freisin. = It's nice to meet you as well.
(leat = leatsa = with_you, freisin = too, also)


(((1.8)
mé = I, me
tú = you
sé (é) = he, it
si (í) = she, it

muid = us, we
sibh = you (plural)
siad = they

Tá mé go maith. = I'm well.
Tá sé go breá. = He's fine.

(((1.11)
Éanna: Slán leat, a Éilís. Feicfidh mé amárach tú. = Goodbye Éilís. I'll see you tomorrow. (feicfidh = see+will, amárach = tomorrow)

Éilís: Slán agat, a Éanna. Tóg go bog é! = Goodbye Éanna. Take it easy!
(tóg=climb, go_bóg=soft,easy, é=it,he)

Éanna: Slán leat, a Éilís. Feicfidh mé amárach tú. = Goodbye Éilís. I'll see you tomorrow. (leat = with_you)

Éilís: Slán go fóill, a Éanna. Tóg go bog é. = Goodbye Éanna. Take it easy!
(fóill=for_now)



(((Conversation 1C)
Caitlín: Seo é Barry. = This is Barry.
Barry: Tá sé go deas bualadh leat, a Nóra. = It's nice to meet you Nóra.
/... Jest to(on) milo spotkac z_toba .../

Caitlín: Seo í Laura. = This is Laura.
Nóra: Cén chaoi a bhfuil tú? = How are you?

Caitlín: Agus seo iad Stiofán agus Peadar. = And this is Stiofán and Peadar.
Nóra: Cén chaoi a bhfuil sibh? = How are you (plural)?



(((Conversation 1D)
Séamas: Slán leat, a Shiobhán. Beidh mé ag caint leat amárach. 
/....Be me kancz lath amarah./ (beidth = will {..dth = future}
= Goodbye, Siobhán. I'll be talking to you tomorrow.
(caint = talk, speak to; AG_caint = talkING)

Siobhán: Slán agat, a Shéamais, agus go raibh maith agat arís. 
= Goodbye, Séamas, and thanks again.

Séamas: Slán leat, a Mháire. Slán abhaile. = Goodbye Máire. Safe home.
(abhaile = home = baile = town)

Máire: Slán agat, a Shéamais. Feicfidh mé amárach tú.
= Goodbye Séamas. I'll see you tomorrow.


(((1.12)
The vocative case:
a Shéamais  = Séamas
a Shiobhán = Siobhán
a Mháire = Máire


(((1.13)
Cén chaoi a bhfuil sibh? = How are you. (plural)
Cén chaoi a bhfuil tú? = How are you.

(((Exercise 1.7)
1. Hello(formal) = Dia duit.
2. How are you (Connacht dialect)? = Cén chaoi a bhfuil tú?
3. I'm well = Tá me go maith.
4. I'm Sharon = Is mise Sharon.
5. My name is Máirtín = Máirtín is ainm dom.
6. My name is Bríd = Bríd is ainm domsa.
7. What's your name? = Cén t-ainm atá ort? (1.6)

9. It is nice to meet you as well = Tá sé go deas bualadh leatsa freisin. (1.7)
10. Goodbye (said to the person leaving) = Slán leat.
11. Goodbye (said to the person staying) = Slán agat.
12. Goodbye for now = Slán go fóill. (1.11)
13. This is Nóra = Seo(here) í Nóra.
14. This is Seán = Seo é Seán.
