UNIT 1: Meeting People
AONAD 1: Bualadh le Daoine

Key Sounds:

mise (slender s) = I, me
slán (broad s) = goodbye
duit (slender t) = to you
leat (broad t) = with you
muid (slender d) = we, us
siad (broad d) = they

(1.1) 
Dia duit = Hello (literally, God to you)
Dia is Muire duit = Hello (literally, God and Mary to you)
Dia daoibh = Hello (greeting more then one person)

(1.2)
Cén chaoi a bhfuil tú? = How are you?
Cad é mar atá tú? = How are you? (Ulster dialect)
Conas atá tú? = How are you? (Munster dialect)

(1.3)
Tá mé go maith. = I'm well
Tá mé go breá. = I'm fine

(1.4)
Is mise Sharon. = I'm Sharon.
Is mise Dónall. = I'm Dónall.
Mise Siobhán = I'm Siobhán

(1.5)
Máirtín is ainm dom. = My name is Máirtín. (dom = me)
Sinéad is ainm dom. = My name is Sinéad.
Peadar is ainm domsa. = My name is Peadar. (domsa = to me)
Agus Bríd is ainm domsa. = And my name is Bríd. 

(1.6)
Cén t-ainm atá ort? = What's your name (Connacht dialect)
Cad is ainm duit? = What's your name (Munster dialect)
C'ainm atá ort? = What's your name (Ulster dialect)

Máirtín is ainm domsa. Cén t-ainm atá ortsa? (ortsa = to you)

(Conversation 1A)
Michelle: Cén chaoi a bhfuil tú? Is mise Michelle - cén t-ainm atá ortsa?
 = How are you? I'm Michelle - what's your name?
 
Eoin: Mise Eoin. = I'm Eoin.

Michelle: Tá sé go deas bualadh leat, a Eoin. = It's nice to meet you Eoin.

(Conversation 1B)
Áine: Dia duit, a Liam. = Hello Liam.

Liam: Dia is Muire duit, a Áine. Cén chaoi a bhfuil tú na laethanta seo? 
= Hello Áine. How are you these days? (lea = day, laethanta = days)

Áine: Tá mé go maith, go raibh maith agat. Agus tú féin?
= I'm well, thank you. And yourself?

Liam: Tá mé go breá. Tá sé go deas tú a fheiceáil arís!
=I'm fine. It's nice to see you again (fheiceáil = to see)

(1.7)
Tá sé go deas bualadh leat, a Eoin. = It's nice to meet you Eoin.

Tá sé go deas bualadh leatsa freisin. = It's nice to meet you as well.
(leat = leatsa = with you)

(1.8)
mé = I, me
tú = you
sé = he, it
si = she, it

muid = us, we
sibh = you (plural)
siad = they

Tá mé go maith. = I'm well.
Tá mé go breá. = I'm fine.
